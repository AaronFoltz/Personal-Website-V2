Clone the NVM repository

git clone git://github.com/creationix/nvm.git ~/.nvm

Add to ~/.bash_profile

. ~/.nvm/nvm.sh

Exit and restart your current terminal.

Other than that, you can follow the instructions at http://carlosedp.com/posts/nvm-for-nodeists-manage-your-install.html to manage your local Node.js installations.
