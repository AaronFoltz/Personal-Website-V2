On the way back home, we ended up driving around randomly, coming upon a couple of amazing "villages" in Hillsboro and Waterford.  The best of the two, Waterford, is a quaint little village with tons of charm.  Most of the streets weren't even adequate enough for two lane driving, definitely giving us a blast from the past!
