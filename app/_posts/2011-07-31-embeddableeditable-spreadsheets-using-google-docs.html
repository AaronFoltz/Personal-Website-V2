Make your spreadsheet public

Click on   -> Share to first make the spreadsheet public.  

 Publish your spreadsheet

  1. Click on   -> Publish as a web page.
  2. Change from "Web Page" to "HTML to embed in a page"
  3. Copy the HTML iframe into your webpage.

Gather the editable link

  1. Click on   -> Share.
  2. Copy the link under "Link to share (allows editing)
  3. Paste the link into the "src" under the iframe copied into your website in the Publish your spreadsheet section above.
